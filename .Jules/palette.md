## 2026-03-30 - [Enhanced Accessibility for Navigation and Forms]
**Learning:** Icon-only buttons and dropdown menus in the current implementation lack necessary ARIA labels and state attributes (aria-expanded, aria-haspopup), making them difficult to navigate for screen reader users.
**Action:** Always include ARIA labels for icon-only buttons and manage expanded/haspopup states for dropdowns to ensure a truly inclusive experience.
